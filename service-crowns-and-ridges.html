Crowns & Bridges

Restore Strength and Function to Your Teeth

Crowns and bridges are used to repair damaged teeth and replace missing ones. At The Dental Edge, we provide well-fitted, natural-looking restorations that help you eat, speak, and smile comfortably.

Dental Crowns

Covers and protects a damaged tooth, restoring its strength and shape.

Dental Bridges

Fills the gap of missing teeth using support from nearby teeth.

Ceramic Crowns

Tooth-colored crowns designed to look natural and blend well.

Zirconia Crowns

Strong and durable crowns suitable for long-term use.

Bridge Replacement

Repair or replace old or damaged dental bridges.

Tooth Restoration

Restore broken or worn-down teeth for better function.

Full Coverage Crowns

Complete protection for weak or heavily damaged teeth.

Consultation & Planning

Proper evaluation to choose the right type of crown or bridge.

Temporary Crowns

Short-term protection for your tooth while the final crown is being prepared.

Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

When do I need a crown?
When a tooth is damaged, weak, or after root canal treatment.
What is a dental bridge used for?
It is used to replace one or more missing teeth.
Are crowns and bridges long-lasting?
Yes, with proper care, they can last for many years.
Will it look natural?
Yes, crowns and bridges are designed to match your natural teeth.
Is the procedure painful?
The treatment is done with care to keep you comfortable.
