Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a root canal?
If you have continuous tooth pain, sensitivity, or swelling, it’s best to get it checked.
Is root canal treatment painful?
No, the procedure is done with care to minimize discomfort.
How long does the treatment take?
It usually takes 1–2 visits depending on the condition of the tooth.
Can I eat after the procedure?
Yes, but it’s advised to avoid hard foods for some time.
Will the tooth function normally after treatment?
Yes, with proper care, the treated tooth works like a normal tooth.
