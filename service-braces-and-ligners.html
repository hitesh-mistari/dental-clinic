Braces & Aligners Treatments We Offer

Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does braces treatment take?
It usually takes several months to a couple of years, depending on the case.
Are braces painful?
You may feel slight discomfort initially, but it adjusts within a few days.
Can adults get braces or aligners?
Yes, both options are suitable for adults as well.
Which is better: braces or aligners?
It depends on your condition and preference. We guide you on the best option.
Do I need to wear retainers after treatment?
Yes, retainers help keep your teeth in their new position.
