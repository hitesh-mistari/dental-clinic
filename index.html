Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?
It’s generally recommended to visit every 6 months for a check-up and cleaning, unless advised otherwise.
What should I do in a dental emergency?
Contact the clinic as soon as possible. Early treatment helps prevent the issue from getting worse.
Do you treat children as well?
Yes, we provide gentle and comfortable dental care for children of all ages.
What are the options for missing teeth?
Depending on your case, options include dental implants, bridges, or other suitable treatments.
Is teeth whitening safe?
Yes, when done under professional guidance, it is safe and effective.
